Site Plan - Audience

Target Audience

The targeted audience for Scoots are individuals that need transportation on Cozumel, Mexico. The major audience are the tourist that visit and don’t already have a means of transportation.

  • Who: The site is for both males and females who have access to the internet.
  • Who: Those the need transportation on the island.
  • Age: 18+
  • Income: All incomes

Since Scoots is in the business of renting vehicles, individuals targeted will be 18 years old or more with a valid driver’s license. Since the biggest targeted audience is tourists, Scoots and the site will cater to both men and women, regardless of race.
